Hi there! I’m an Applied Scientist at Microsoft AI. I completed my Master’s at the Language Technologies Institute, Carnegie Mellon University and earned my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering at PES University.

In 2024, I interned at Microsoft AI, where I utilized Bayesian hyperparameter optimization and network architecture search techniques to optimize model performance and size. In 2023, I interned at Apple and developed a few-shot multi-label text classifier and semi-automated data annotation.

My research interests are broadly efficieny and interpretability in machine learning. I briefly worked with Prof. Emma Strubell on ways to reuse parameters while growing and fine-tuning models. I also worked with Prof. Yonatan Bisk on evaluating the semantic understanding capabilities of text-to-image generation models. Previously, I worked on various other projects and you can find a list of my publications and my resume.
